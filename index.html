Globetrotters®

Globetrotters® is a full-service professional organization based in Chicago that has served the public and private sectors since 1974 in the areas of design, management, and consulting. With 200+ personnel, Globetrotters® draws upon highly qualified and experienced staff with diverse expertise to provide cost-effective, sustainable, and responsive services to its clients.

Services Offered

Globetrotters provides architecture, engineering, construction management, permit plan reviews, facility and property management, consulting, and IT services. Globetrotters is committed to planning, designing, constructing, and managing quality and sustainable infrastructure projects that play an integral role in the development and success of the communities it serves.
