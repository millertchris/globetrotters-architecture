University Center

Lake County, Illinois

Client Name

University Center

Services Used

Mechanical Engineering
Electrical Engineering
Plumbing Engineering
Fire Protection Engineering

Globetrotters provided total mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection engineering services for the new University Center, which consists of a 91,000 SF facility on the College of Lake County Grayslake Campus. The $25.6 million facility houses classrooms to support the programs offered by the University Center’s 17 academic partners.

The new facility has distance learning classrooms, seminar rooms, computer labs, an electronic library, and a conference wing with a lecture hall and executive tiered classrooms. It is equipped with state-of-the-art, ADA compliant technology to support the latest learning trends in higher education.

In 2006, the Capital Development Board awarded the University Center the Thomas H. Madigan Award for Outstanding New Construction.
