Permit Review

Globetrotters has performed building permit plan reviews to ensure municipal code compliance since the City of Chicago’s program was outsourced in 2003. A full complement of professional disciplines is provided by Globetrotters to meet the permit review process objectives, including: Architectural, Plumbing, Electrical, Ventilation, Refrigeration, Fire Prevention, Accessibility, Environmental, Sewer, Water, Structural Foundations & Retention Systems Globetrotters’ staff also perform independent reviews of other consultants’ plans as part of the Department of Buildings’ Structural Peer Review Program.

Permit Review Services Include:

  • Small to Medium Projects (Standard Plan)
  • Medium to Large or Complex Projects (Developer Services)
  • Foundation and Superstructure
  • Core and Shell
