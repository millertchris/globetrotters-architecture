Ajay Shah, Esq.

CEO

Ajay is the CEO of Globetrotters Engineering Corporation. Ajay became CEO in August of 2017 after having worked as Chief Operating Officer and General Counsel of the Corporation for several years. In this role Ajay provides the strategic vision and the leadership in execution of the firm’s goals.

As CEO, Ajay Shah is ultimately responsible for executing the vision of the company. Mr. Shah is an attorney in Illinois. Mr. Shah received his Bachelor’s of Science in Mathematics from Purdue University at West Lafayette. Mr. Shah then obtained his Juris Doctor degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Most recently, Mr. Shah completed the OPM 49 program at Harvard Business School.
